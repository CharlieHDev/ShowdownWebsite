Spleef meets madness! Vote on 5 different spleef types with different item sets and item boxes, look where you walk and don't fall!



Watch the explanation




3 rounds of spleef gameplay, compete to be the last team standing in different renditions of spleef modes.


Map Voting



Before each round starts, all players can vote for which spleef mode they wish to play next by standing in the area of the spleef mode they desire. Most votes win.


🪏 Classic Spleef



Grab your shovel, and mine the blocks below you to gain snowballs. Snowballs can be thrown at the floor to destroy blocks they hit.

Power-ups


  • Speed Potion (10s)

  • Jump Potion (10s)


🎣 Slippery Spleef



With a pickaxe to mine the ice, and a fishing rod to hook onto other players and launch them, watch your step its slippery!

Power-ups


  • Speed Potion (10s)

  • Jump Potion (10s)


🔥 Nether Spleef



The nether is the map, with a shovel and a pickaxe you must mine both nether blocks and soul soil. Gain soul speed running on soul soil with your soul speed boots. Shoot fireballs at blocks to destroy them.

Power-ups


  • Speed Potion (10s)

  • Jump Potion (10s)

  • Projectile Fireballs


👁️ Ender Spleef



With your pickaxe and enderpearls you must remain on top, clutch up by pearling to safety but be sure to stay accurate else you may miss the platforms!

Power-ups


  • Speed Potion (10s)

  • Jump Potion (10s)

  • Enderpearls


🥾 Copper Spleef



No tools this time, only copper decay boots and a mace! Walk across the copper to slowly decay it, the copper has 4-decay levels.. starting orange and oxidizing to blue and eventually disappearing. Additionally, the mace and wind charges can be used to perform a smash attack which can send players flying while also decaying a radius beneath them.

Power-ups


  • Speed Potion (10s)

  • Jump Potion (10s)

  • Infinite Wind Charges
