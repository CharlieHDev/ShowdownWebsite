It's all about crafting! Speak to Edguard to get a list of required items to craft, look at the map to find out which tunnels to go through to find those items.



Watch the explanation




10 minutes of pure crafting, speak to Edguard in the main castle room and he will give you instructions on what items you are required to craft.

All materials gathered within the game are kept, inventories are not cleared once you've crafted an item, so you may even stock up on materials!


🗺️ The Tunnel System



The materials required to craft items are scattered in the castle sewer system, use the tunnel map located in the main castle room with Edguard.

The tunnels are filled with water, use your trusty Trident to riptide quickly to your destination.

The tunnels are marked with a colour systems, there are 4 main tunnels which then split off into 2 separate tunnels.

Here is an example of what the map looks like:



For example, if you wanted to reach the sheep section, you'd traverse through the Aqua tunnel and then through the Purple tunnel.


🚥 3-tier crafting



Once you've spoken to Edguard, you will be given three different items to craft. Easy, Medium, and Hard.


Difficulties


  • Easy - Straight forward crafts, usually require 1 tunnel to traverse to.

  • Medium - Slightly more difficult, may require two tunnels to traverse.

  • Hard - Hardest crafts, require minimum two tunnels to traverse, more complicated crafts.


You are also able to craft multiple items at once! You can choose to craft any combination of items you want which may cut time in the long-haul! Are the easy and medium items in similar tunnels? Give Edguard both!

You can even craft all three difficulties at once!


🪙 Points



🪙 +15 Points per Easy craft.

🪙 +25 Points per Medium craft.

🪙 +35 Points per Hard craft.
