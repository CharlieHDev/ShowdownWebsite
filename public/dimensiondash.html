Speed is key. Race against your opponents, travel across dimensions, encounter different tests of movement skill including elytra, trident, and movement pads.



Watch the explanation




Portals have opened across the park, and one specific portal located in The Journey can take you wherever you want to go!

Be as quick as you can and utilise different movement types to secure first place.. including trident, elytra, launch/speed/jump pads, swimming, parkour, and more!


🌌 The Portal



The portal located at the centre of the map is your ticket to new dimensions...

The player who reaches the portal first will decide where players are taken next, there are two paths when you reach the portal indicated by the blocks on the floor and the name of the dimension above the area.

Run across the dimension you want to travel to, the player who reaches the portal first will change the portals destination for all players, so be quick!


Dimensions


  • 2023 - We're going back to OG Showdown, travel to the 2023 event world and race through memories.

  • Ski Resort - A ski resort located next to a mountain has appeared, run around the mountain, climb it, then traverse into it before reaching the summit.

  • Chaos Canyon - The canyon is long and winding, traverse the terrain and reach the river at the end of the canyon!


⛰️ Back to the park!



This game consists of two rounds, the first round you will travel forward... then in the second round you will play the map in reverse.

The map will change slightly for both rounds, with different paths for both rounds follow the arrows to assist you in your journey!
