Speed is key. Race against your opponents, travel across dimensions, encounter different tests of movement skill including elytra, trident, and movement pads.



Watch the explanation




3 rounds of sumo gameplay, compete to be the last team standing in a high octane knockback based game. Hit off opponents from platforms using your knockback stick and double jump by double tapping space bar to traverse islands.

Double jump resets once you landed on an island, so be careful with your movements!


🗺️ Maps and Modes



In 3 rounds you will traverse 2 maps, the 1st round will be in Adrenaline Ravine, the 2nd and 3rd round will be in Desert. The 3rd round will involve Ice Platforms where movements will be a little more slippery!


Maps


  • Adrenaline Ravine - Straight forward movement, find your bearings.

  • Desert - Circle round the map clockwise, smaller islands and a closer matchup.


🏝️ Crumbling Islands



Overtime, islands will spawn, but also islands will crumble. Islands will spawn in the direction of the map, and the ones behind will slowly decay. Crumbling islands will turn red to signify it will shortly be destroyed. So move quickly and avoid being stuck!


❤️ Lives



Each round players will start with ❤️ 2 lives. If a player falls with 2 lives they will respawn on a random safe space on active islands.

Immunity will be granted to the fallen player for 5 seconds (or until they hit another player). The fallen player will not be attackable during this time and will be identified by equipped iron armour.

If you fall during your immunity period you will be teleported back to a random active island.


Life Cap


Halfway through the round, a life cap will be enabled after a 5 second countdown. Players who have ❤️ 2 lives during this life cap will be capped at ❤️ 1 life and given 🪙 +20 Points.

Players who have ❤️ 1 life during the life cap will be unaffected.


🪙 Points



🪙 +16 Points per player final elimination.

🪙 +4 Points per player elimination.

🪙 +3 Points per player outlived.

🪙 Individual points per end of round placement, minus 1 per placement. (1st - 32pts > 32nd - 1pt)
