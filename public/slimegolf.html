You and your team are tasked with putting the slime across the course as quickly as you can, finding shortcuts and calculating the best possible route to reach the finish.



Watch the explanation




Use your knockback stick, fishing rod, and double jump to punch the large slime across this maze of different paths and challenges to reach the hole as quickly as possible.

Look for shortcuts, and think strategically as a team to push the slime as fast as possible through the courses..


🗺️ Maps and Modes



In 3 rounds you will traverse 2 maps, the 1st round will be in Slimey Slipway, the 2nd and 3rd round will be in Haunted Hallways. The 3rd round will involve Modifiers where different events will throw you off your game!


Maps


  • Slimey Slipway - A classic map across a green pathway, traverse tunnels, mazes, bubble columns, and stay on the platforms.

  • Haunted Hallways - A spooky new addition which traverses a haunted mansion, courtyard, graveyard, and into a skull.

Modifiers


  • Thunderstorm - Rain falls, lightning will strike your slime and send it flying into the air every so often.

  • Honey I shrunk the roster! - All players are shrunk down in size! Making it harder to traverse the course.


🔷 Player-only Shortcuts



Throughout the courses you will find player-only shortcuts which will be labelled with a light blue concrete flag.

These shortcuts will allow players to get ahead of the slime and prepare themselves to punch the slime further and faster! Think strategically and communicate with your teammates to decide how best to advance the course and hit the slime as quickly as you can to the hole.
