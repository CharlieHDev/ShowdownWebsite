A round robin game where your objective is to control 3 points, pushing as far as you can before the factory overloads!



Watch the explanation




It's a game of tug of war! Each team is thrown into a team versus team arena, each team facing every other team once each.

The objective of this mode is to control moving walls:


⚔️ PvP Gameplay



This is a PvP mode, to control the points you will have to fight for it.

Players also respawn when eliminated, respawns take 5 seconds where you are given the choice of different respawn locations.

A menu will open upon death which allows you to choose what part of the arena to respawn in: All 3 sites, and the base.


⚔️ Kits


Your team has a choice from a selection of kits to use to battle your opponents.

Each player can choose 1 unique kit out of 5 options:

  • Tank - Iron Chestplate, Iron Boots, Wooden Sword, Crossbow (Quick Charge II), Arrows x8

  • Archer - Chainmail Chestplate, Iron Boots, Wooden Sword, Crossbow (Quick Charge II), Arrows x6

  • Duelist - Chainmail Chestplate, Iron Boots, Stone Sword, Bow, Arrows

  • Healer - Chainmail Chestplate, Iron Boots, Wooden Sword, Bow, Splash Healing Potion, Splash Regeneration Potion, Golden Apple, Arrows

  • Flanker - Leather Chestplate, Iron Boots, Stone Sword, Bow, Swiftness Potion x2, Golden Apple, Arrows

Each player will be given the option to select a kit before the round starts, if they do not choose a kit one will be automatically granted.

You can re-open the kit menu by pressing your off-hand keybind before the round starts.


🚗 The Walls



Across the arena, there are 3 different walls for players to control and push. Each wall has a percentage counter above them which indicates how much land gained both teams have.

To move these walls, simply stand near them and the wall will move automatically every 0.5s as long as you are within a 3-block radius of the wall so stay aware at all times!


➗ Scoring


At the end of the round, each walls total percentage for both teams is calculated into one average sum to determine who gained the most land.

If the percentages are as follows:

  • Wall A - Ruby 75% | 25% Topaz

  • Wall B - Ruby 40% | 60% Topaz

  • Wall C - Ruby 65% | 35% Topaz

Then the final scores would be Ruby 60% | 40% Topaz, resulting in a win for Team Ruby.

Score formula: (A + B + C) ÷ 3


👥 Movement Multiplier


The amount of players pushing a wall will determine the distance at which the wall moves at a time.

Movement multipliers are as follows:

Players Pushing Multiplier
1 1.0x
2 1.7x
3 2.2x
4 2.5x

Depending on how many players from both teams are pushing a wall at a time can change the distance the wall moves.

When a wall is contested, the movements are reduced by how many players are contesting the point.

For example, if 2 players from Ruby are pushing and 1 player from Topaz is pushing then Ruby will only push the wall 1 players worth of distance at a time.

If there is an equal amount of players from both teams pushing a wall, then the wall will not move at all while contested.


🏃 Final Push


In the final 30 seconds, wall movements are increased. Making late clutches even more exciting and important.

Additionally, respawn times are reduced to 3 seconds during this period.

The movement multipliers during final push are as follows:

Players Pushing Multiplier
1 1.2x
2 1.9x
3 2.4x
4 2.8x
