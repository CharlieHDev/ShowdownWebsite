Work as a team to build and replicate 6 bridge pieces. After each build, you will be made to parkour across each build you've just made.



Watch the explanation




In this game, you and your team will work to complete the course as fast as you can.

The mode is split into 2 sections:


Build Mode



Every player will be put into build mode, where your objective is to copy the build on the right side into the area on the left side. Each build will be comprised of maximum 9 blocks which will be provided to you in your inventory.

Blocks can be insta-broken when left-clicked, any incorrect block placements will be highlighted with a red glow helping you see where mistakes have been made.

Once your team has successfully replicated the build, the parkour mode will begin.


Parkour Mode



Once the build is complete, your team will be made invisible and a 3-second countdown will start. The build will generate itself before you on the bridge.

You and your team will have 30 seconds to complete this parkour jump. Points are awarded for each player who completes the jump, with a bonus reward for a full team completion.

If the jump is fully complete, or the 30 second timer runs out, the next build mode will commence.


🪙 Points



🪙 +25 Points per player who completes a jump.

🪙 +20 Points for a full team completion of a jump, split amongst the team.

🪙 +100 points split amongst the team after completing a build, minus 6 points per your build time placement calculated after the game is finished. (e.g. 1st - 100pts, 2nd - 94pts)
